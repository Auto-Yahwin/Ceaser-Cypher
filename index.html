What Is a Cypher?

A Cypher is a secret or disguised way of writing where the characters are encoded in a given format. Read more on Cyphers

How does this work?

The Application takes in inputs and replaces each alphabetic character with another character that is at a certain position(key) down the alphabeth. Non-alphabetic characters remains unaffected.
For example, with 3 as the key, 'A' will be encode to 'D' while 'K' will encode to 'N'
